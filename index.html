About Me

I am a mission-driven leader passionate about empowering teams, harnessing technology, and creating lasting impact. My journey began at the Eastern Mediterranean International School (EMIS), where I completed an International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma with a focus on economics and global politics. Following high school, I enlisted in the Israeli Defense Forces (IDF), earning my commission as a Combat ICT Officer. In that capacity, I managed critical communication systems, oversaw operational planning, and refined my leadership skills under high-pressure conditions. Later, I served in a classified command and technology role in the 8200-Signal Intelligence Unit, broadening my technical expertise. After completing my service, I returned to EMIS as a Boarding School Social Guide and also led digital transformation projects as an Automation Manager. Currently pursuing a bachelor's in Business Administration and Entrepreneurship at Reichman University, I remain committed to bridging innovation, education, and leadership to drive positive change.
